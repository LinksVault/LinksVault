Delete Your Account

LinksVault by Yonatan Carmeli

⚠️ Important: This Action Cannot Be Undone

Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data from LinksVault. This action cannot be reversed.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your account and authentication information
  • All your collections and albums
  • All saved links and bookmarks
  • Profile information (name, email, profile photo)
  • All uploaded images and media
  • All app preferences and settings
  • All associated metadata and timestamps

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the LinksVault app on your device
  2. Navigate to the Profile screen (usually accessible from the main menu or bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" option
  4. Tap on "Delete Account"
  5. You will see a warning message explaining what will be deleted
  6. Confirm your decision by tapping "Delete"
  7. You will be asked to confirm again - tap "Yes, Delete My Account"
  8. Wait for the deletion process to complete (this may take a few moments)
  9. You will be automatically signed out and returned to the login screen

Data Retention

Once you delete your account:

  • All data is permanently deleted immediately
  • There is no retention period - data cannot be recovered
  • Backup copies (if any) are also deleted within 30 days
